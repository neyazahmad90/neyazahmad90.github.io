How we use cookies
A cookie is a small file which needs prior approval for storing it on your computer’s hard drive. The cookie is added only with your consent and this file assists to analyze web traffic or lets you know when you visit a particular site. Cookies permit web applications to react with you like an individual. The web application can customize its operations as per your requirements, likes and dislikes by gathering and remembering information about your preferences.
We use traffic log cookies to identify which pages are visited or used. This helps us to analyze the data related to webpage traffic and improve our website in accordance with our customer’s needs. We use this information only or statistical analysis; and afterwards the data is removed from the system.
Generally, cookies enable us to provide you with a better website, by allowing us to monitor which pages are useful to you and also which pages are useless. A cookie does not grant us access to your computer or any information about you; other than the data you choose to share with us. You can choose to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, although you can easily alter your browser setting to reject cookies as per your preference. However, this may prevent you from obtaining full advantage of the website.
