Over 65
Years of Expertise
One man’s pain has a lot of power, power to turn it into a blessing for many. It is a quality of great men to see beyond one’s own personal gain and go ahead on a path of greater good. On 12th September, 1920, our founding father, Dr. P Siva Reddy was born. At the tender age of 9, he witnessed the excruciating experience that blindness was, with his mother fighting glaucoma; he chose to pursue his career in medicine, eventually ophthalmology.

Of all the love in the world, a mother’s love is the strongest, and with her blessing from the beyond Dr. P Siva Reddy created his legacy of healing. Today he regarded with great respect for his contributions. Dr P Siva Reddy’s story is that of sheer dedication, hard work and sincerity toward his vision for a world that is cured of treatable blindness. He was honoured with Padmabushan in 1977, Padmashri in 1971, and B.C Roy Awards in 1981 and again in 1987. Dr. Reddy was appointed as Honorary Surgeon to the President of India, Sri V.V.Giri, in which capacity he served the chair for 25 years. Padmabushan Dr Perugu Siva Reddy Regional Eye Hospital, Kurnool, was named in his honour by the Government of Andhra Pradesh, in recognition of his services.

After his retirement from formal duties, he continued to follow his religion of serving people and instituted Dr. P Siva Reddy Hospital in Hyderabad with a single consultation room. After 3 generations his legacy has evolved into a State-Of-Art Advanced Surgical and Laser Eye Care Centre. Today the institution is in the able hands of Dr. P Siva Reddy’s grandson, Dr. H Siva Mohan Reddy. Under the wise counsel of his father Dr. H Prabhakar Reddy and inspiration from his grandfather’s life of service and dedication, Dr. H Siva Mohan Reddy took it upon himself to upgrade the medical facilities at PSR Hospital to the latest technology and best of amenities.

Dr. P Siva Reddy’s legacy lies in the memories of his patients as a doctor who truly cared; this legacy has been nurtured over 3 generations bringing forth a sincere health care facility that PSR Eye Hospital is today.
