OVER 65 YEARS OF
EXPERTISE IN CATARACT

This hospital, established by Padmabushan Dr. P Siva Reddy, is a result of legacy that has been nurtured over 3 generations bringing forth a sincere health care facility that PSR Eye Hospital is today. This hospital is a result of sheer dedication, hard work and sincerity of Dr. P Siva Reddy (the founding father), Dr. H Prabhakar Reddy and grandson Dr. H Siva Mohan Reddy. Dr P Siva Reddy’s story is that of sheer dedication, hard work and sincerity toward his vision for a world that is cured of treatable blindness. He was honoured with Padmabushan in 1977, Padmashri in 1971, and B.C Roy Awards in 1981 and again in 1987. Dr. Reddy was appointed as Honorary Surgeon to the President of India, Sri V.V.Giri, in which capacity he served the chair for 25 years. Padmabushan Dr Perugu Siva Reddy Regional Eye Hospital, Kurnool, was named in his honour by the Government of Andhra Pradesh, in recognition of his services.
After his retirement from formal duties, he continued to follow his religion of serving people and instituted Dr. P Siva Reddy Hospital in Hyderabad with a single consultation room. After 3 generations his legacy has evolved into a State-Of-Art Advanced Surgical and Laser Eye Care Centre.
Today the institution is in the able hands of Dr. P Siva Reddy’s grandson, Dr. H Siva Mohan Reddy. Under the wise counsel of his father Dr. H Prabhakar Reddy and inspiration from his grandfather’s life of service and dedication, Dr. H Siva Mohan Reddy took it upon himself to upgrade the medical facilities at PSR Hospital to the latest technology and best of amenities. Dr. P Siva Reddy’s legacy lies in the memories of his patients as a doctor who truly cared; this legacy has been nurtured over 3 generations bringing forth a sincere health care facility that PSR Eye Hospital is today.
